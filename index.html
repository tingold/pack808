    • About Us

    • Our Base

      Cub Scout Pack 808 is chartered by First Presbyterian Church, located at 224 N Main St. Bel Air, MD 21014 - across the street from Shoprite. We are a family friendly Pack; siblings, parents, and grandparents are welcome to attend meetings and events.

    • Pack Meetings

      These meetings are held on the third Wednesday of the month at 7pm at First Presbyterian. All Dens attend the Pack meetings. Pack meetings consist of an opening flag ceremony, advancement recognition, and group games. During nice weather, we try to hold as many meetings outside as possible.

    • Pack Dues

      Dues are $95 per year. These dues are due by the end of November and cover patches, rank badges, beads, pins, belt loops, pack meetings food and activities and various administrative supplies. We do not require our families to sell popcorn as most local Packs do.

    • Pack Structure

      The Pack has a committee consisting of the Cubmaster, treasurer, secretary, advancement chair, and leaders of each den. The committee handles administrative and support tasks that allow the Cubmaster, den leaders, and their assistants to focus on working directly with the Cub Scouts.

    • Volunteering

      Everyone is welcome and encouraged to attend committee meetings, they are held the first and third Mondays of the month at Buontempo Brothers Pizza. It's a great way to get involved with the pack and help make you son's experience the best it can be

    • Safety

      All leaders go through role specific training to ensure that your sons are safe at all times. Additionally all adults in the pack have to complete Youth Proctection Training.
